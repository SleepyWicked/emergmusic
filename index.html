The Artist

Born &
Built in
Pilsen

Emerg is a Chicago rapper and storyteller rooted in the Pilsen neighborhood on the city's Lower West Side — one of Chicago's most culturally vibrant Latino communities. His music is the sound of that block: raw, melodic, proud, and uncompromising.

Drawing from the Chicano Rap and Latin Hip-Hop tradition, Emerg weaves vivid streetscapes with personal truth, honoring his roots while carving out a lane that's entirely his own. Every bar is a postcard from Pilsen.

93
The Year Everything Started
The year Emerg was born in Chicago. The same year Wicked Entertainment Network was founded by Sleepy and Sinbad. That alignment isn't coincidence — it's destiny written into the music.
18
Miles Apart, Same City
Emerg hails from Pilsen. Producers Crook County (Marcos Martinez & Dave Dillion) are from Little Village — two neighboring Chicago communities bound by culture, familia, and the same streets.
CH
Chicano Rap Roots
Rooted in the Chicano Rap and Latin Hip-Hop tradition, Wicked Since 93 is a debut album that honors where this music came from — and points toward where it's going.
IL
Pilsen's Own
Chicago's Pilsen neighborhood has always been a creative epicenter. Emerg carries that identity everywhere — it's in the name, the logo, and every bar he drops.
